# Privacy Policy for Bits Today

**Effective date:** July 23, 2026

Bits Today is a private, single-user application used by its developer, Ahnaf
Hassan, to create, review, and publish content to Facebook Pages and Instagram
accounts managed by the developer. Bits Today does not offer public
registration or public user accounts.

## 1. Information processed

Bits Today may process the following information when it connects to supported
services:

- Facebook and Instagram account, Page, and media identifiers;
- access tokens and authentication credentials issued by Meta;
- Page or account names and publishing-related information;
- images, videos, captions, descriptions, and other content selected for
  generation, review, or publication; and
- publishing results and technical information, including post identifiers,
  URLs, status responses, and API errors.

Bits Today does not intentionally collect personal information from members of
the public.

## 2. How information is used

The information described above is processed only to:

- authenticate with Facebook and Instagram;
- create, preview, publish, and manage content on accounts controlled by the
  developer;
- generate social-media text and images;
- deliver private preview packages to the developer; and
- check publishing status, maintain security, and diagnose technical errors.

## 3. Storage and retention

Application credentials and access tokens are stored locally in protected
environment configuration. They are not intentionally included in public
source code.

Generated media, captions, publishing identifiers, and diagnostic information
may be retained on the developer's local systems for as long as reasonably
necessary to operate, secure, and troubleshoot Bits Today. Locally retained
information is deleted when it is no longer required or following a valid
deletion request, unless retention is required by law.

## 4. Third-party services

Bits Today communicates with the following service providers when required by
the workflow:

- **Meta**, for Facebook and Instagram authentication and publishing;
- **OpenAI**, for generating text and images;
- **Telegram**, for sending private preview packages to the developer; and
- **FxTwitter/FxEmbed**, for retrieving publicly available X/Twitter post
  information.

Information submitted to these services is handled under their respective
terms and privacy policies. Bits Today does not control the independent data
retention or processing practices of these third parties.

## 5. Data sharing and sale

The developer does not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information
is disclosed only to the service providers identified above when necessary to
operate Bits Today, when directed by the developer, or when required by law.

## 6. Data security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect
credentials and locally retained information. Access tokens and application
secrets are kept outside public source code. However, no method of electronic
storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 7. Data deletion and revocation

Bits Today does not maintain public user profiles. A person may request
deletion of information associated with the application by following the
instructions in [DATA_DELETION.md](DATA_DELETION.md) or by emailing
[ahnafhassan6516@gmail.com](mailto:ahnafhassan6516@gmail.com).

Access granted to Bits Today may also be revoked through the relevant Facebook
or Instagram account settings. Revocation prevents future access but may not
automatically delete information already retained locally or content already
published to an account.

## 8. Children's privacy

Bits Today is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal
information from children.

## 9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the application's functionality or
data-processing practices change. Material revisions will be reflected by an
updated effective date at the top of this document.
